Chapter 01 // Released March 19, 2025

The Obsessive Shadow

Survival Horror // First-Person // VR + Flat
Available Now

The Obsessive Shadow is a survival horror game about a stalker you can't quite identify. With nothing but your eyes and a flashlight at your disposal, it's up to you to survive whatever horrific person or thing is out there watching you.

You take on the role of Liam — a 9-year-old boy left alone in his home after a sudden power outage plunges the house into suffocating darkness. Your mother's voice on the phone is your only guide. But as her instructions grow more confusing and the unease deepens, even her words begin to feel fragile.

Short Film Adaptation // Out Now

The Obsessive Shadow
— Official Shortfilm Adaptation

Official Short Film
Out Now

Based on the survival indie horror game, The Obsessive Shadow delivers a tense, atmospheric story of a mother fighting to save her son after a chilling message on TV warns of a stalker in the dark — and a neighbor whose past may hold the key to survival.

When an emergency broadcast disrupts the night, Nicole's (Beth Waugh; Need for Speed, 6 Below) son becomes trapped as something sinister stalks their home. Her neighbor Carl (Joseph Poliquin; Five Nights at Freddy's, Greyhound, The Purge) insists he's come face to face with this evil in the past, leaving Nicole to decide if she can trust him to survive the night.

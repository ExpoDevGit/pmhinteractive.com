Who We Are

Shaping the Future of Indie Games


PMH Interactive is an independent game studio on a mission to jumpstart the next generation of unique and exciting indie games. We're committed to developing immersive and innovative gaming experiences that captivate players and push the boundaries of game design.

We love every platform — from PC and console to mobile and VR. Whether you're gaming on a PS5, Steam Deck, Meta Quest, or Xbox, we're building experiences for everyone.

PlayStation Partner Program certified. Shipping across PS5, PSVR2, Meta Quest, Steam, Xbox, and more. Pure passion is the flesh and bones of this studio.
